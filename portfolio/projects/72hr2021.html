TF2M 72HR JAM 2021

TEAM FORTRESS 2

 
 

This detail map was created for the 2021 installment of TF2Maps' annual 72hr Jam, and even earned a spot in the showcase! Development took place over, of course, a three day period, from fresh VMF to final packed, cubemapped, and repacked compile.

The idea for this environment was a cross between the classic supervillainy 'secret high-tech lair in a jungle' that TF2's 60's-spy-movie aesthetics lend well to, crossed with a showroom - after all, how else would an evil megacorporation secure those juicy contracts?

 

Development screengrabs. The support beams are repurposed parts of arena_nucleus' hero prop!

 

The development, start to finish, was documented in a decently-sized twitter thread. I put a decent amount of effort into the trains that pass along the far wall - there's a spawning system that allows for easily programming in carriage arrangements, the locomotives are wrapped in custom brushwork, and every soundscape in the map switches out to a variation with the train audible whenever it's present.

Though it was pretty exhausting to put this whole thing together in such a short timeframe, it was a ton of fun both to flex some artpassing muscle and to take part in a huge community charity event. This sort of thing is one of the very few exceptions worth crunching for.
