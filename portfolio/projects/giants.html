GIANTS

TEAM FORTRESS 2

 
 

Giants is a rotationally symmetrical Capture the Flag map for Team Fortress 2 set in, vaguely, the snowy permafrost of Iceland. Development took place over two separate periods - the layout was rapidly iterated upon through April-March 2020, and was remade from scratch and artpassed over winter 2021-22.

Giants was originally developed for the Pick It Up! Contest, placing fourth (under a silly working name). Pretty good, considering how new I was to level design at that point!
